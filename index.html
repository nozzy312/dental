About

A decade of clinical excellence, built on genuine care.

Irena is a highly skilled and compassionate dental hygienist with over ten years of clinical experience dedicated to delivering exceptional patient care. Known for her gentle touch and meticulous approach, she has earned the trust and admiration of her patients — many of whom specifically request her for their routine visits.
